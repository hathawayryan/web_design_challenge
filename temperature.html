Latitude vs. Max Temperature

When comparing latitude vs. max temperature, the data shows that typically the closer a city is to a latitude of 0, the warmer it will be. Looking closer at the data it appears that cities in the Southern Hemisphere, with a negative latitude, trend towards being warmer than cities on the opposite side of the equator. This trend isn't as strong as the first one, but when looking for warm weather, the data points towards cities near the equator or just south of it.
