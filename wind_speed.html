Latitude vs. Wind Speed

When comparing latitude vs. wind speed, there appears to be a slight trend towards higher wind speeds at latitudes that are further south. This trend is fairly weak, and it is easy to find cities with a low wind speed at virtually any latitude. One thing to note is that while the trend is to have higher wind speeds when going south, the highest wind speeds observed by the cities are actually far north, at around a latitude of 60. When looking for a city with low wind speeds, the data suggests it would be best to avoid going far north, and instead staying near the equator.
