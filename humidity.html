Latitude vs. Humidity

When comparing latitude vs. humidity, the data shows a small trend towards being more humid when further north, though this trend is not very strong. One thing to notice though is that there are several places with 100% humidity distributed between a latitude of around -45 to 75. There are several points with 0% humidity, and they are all between a latitude of 60 and 75. Overall, if looking for ar city with moderate humidity, it should be easy to find one no matter what latitude is desired.
